## About Growthify

Growthify is a 16-module growth and conversion suite for Shopify merchants, built as a Remix embedded app plus a theme app extension with 72 Liquid blocks. It is part of a broader portfolio of products and open-source packages maintained by Ahsan.

- **Marketing site:** [growthify.aoneahsan.com](https://growthify.aoneahsan.com)
- **Docs source:** [github.com/aoneahsan/all-in-one-shopify-docs](https://github.com/aoneahsan/all-in-one-shopify-docs)
