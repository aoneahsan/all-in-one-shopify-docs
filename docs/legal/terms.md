# Terms (summary)

This page summarises Growthify's terms for documentation readers. The **canonical, legally binding Terms of Service** are published at **https://growthify.aoneahsan.com/terms** and in the app repo's `docs/legal/TERMS-OF-SERVICE.md`.

## Key points

- **Billing** is through Shopify App Subscriptions only. Your Growthify access follows your active Shopify subscription; cancelling in Shopify ends paid access. See [Billing plans](../admin/billing-plans.md).
- **Acceptable use** — you are responsible for the content you publish through Growthify modules (popups, reviews moderation, page-builder pages, messaging) and for complying with the laws that apply to your store.
- **Third-party providers** — features that send email/SMS/WhatsApp use providers you connect with your own credentials; their terms also apply.
- **No warranty of outcomes** — Growthify provides tooling; it does not guarantee specific sales, conversion, or fraud-prevention results. The OTP/COD module reduces, but cannot eliminate, fraudulent orders.
- **License** — the Growthify product is proprietary. The app source repository is published for transparency and security audit only, not for reuse.
