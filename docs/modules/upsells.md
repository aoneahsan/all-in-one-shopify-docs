# Upsells

The upsells module (09) adds offers after the sale, where conversion is high and risk is low.

## What you get

- **Post-purchase / thank-you-page upsells** — a one-click offer shown on the order-status page.
- **Order-status block** — additional content and offers on the post-checkout page.

## Surfaces

- **Checkout UI extensions:** `thank-you-upsell` and `order-status-block` (in the app's `extensions/`, built with `@shopify/ui-extensions-react`).
- **Storefront/admin:** offer configuration in the Upsells module config.

## FAQ

**Where do these appear?**
On Shopify's thank-you and order-status pages via checkout UI extensions — not by editing checkout.liquid.

**Do these require a specific Shopify plan?**
Checkout UI extensions are available on standard Shopify plans for the post-purchase and order-status surfaces; capabilities depend on your Shopify plan.
