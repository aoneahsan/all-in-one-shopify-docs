# Wishlist

The wishlist module (05) lets shoppers save products and come back to them.

## What you get

- **Save-for-later** from product and collection pages.
- **Shareable wishlists** so shoppers can send a list to a friend.
- **Recently viewed** products carousel.

## Surfaces

- **Theme blocks:** `recently-viewed`, `save-for-later`, plus wishlist add/share controls.
- **Storefront API:** wishlist add/remove/list and `GET /apps/growthify/api/storefront/recently-viewed`.
- **Admin:** the Wishlist module config (display, share options).

## FAQ

**Do shoppers need an account to use the wishlist?**
A guest wishlist is keyed to the browser; signed-in customers get a persistent, cross-device list.

**Can shoppers share their wishlist?**
Yes — the share control produces a link that renders the saved items.
